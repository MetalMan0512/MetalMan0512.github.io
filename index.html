About Daniel

Trusted Low Voltage Systems Expert

Daniel Hillhouse is a Senior Project Manager and Low Voltage Systems Specialist with over a decade of hands-on experience delivering complex security and infrastructure projects for enterprise clients across California and throughout the Western and Southern United States — spanning grocery chains, big-box retail, banking, logistics, food service, data centers, and warehousing.

A licensed C-7 Low Voltage Contractor, Daniel has built much of his career as a trusted field lead and subcontractor for some of America's largest security integrators — representing their brand standards across statewide multi-site rollouts with no-rework results. He consistently earns a reputation as the technician teams call when a project needs to be rescued, accelerated, or done right the first time.

From directing multi-year enterprise security overhauls to terminating 10,000+ feet of Cat6 cabling in dense IDF closets, Daniel combines field-level technical mastery with project management discipline, always delivering on time, within budget, and to the strictest compliance standards.

What I Do

Areas of Expertise

A decade of hands-on technical experience across America's most demanding commercial and enterprise environments.

IP Surveillance & Camera Systems

Full-cycle installation and commissioning of IP-based surveillance systems including AXIS, NVR/VMS configuration, Public View Monitor (PVM) setups, and camera FOV optimization for maximum loss prevention coverage.

AXIS Cameras NVR/VMS IP Config Loss Prevention

Intrusion & Access Control

Installation, programming, and hardening of enterprise-grade security panels (DMP, Bosch, Honeywell, Residio). Vault security, motion sensors, door contacts, hold-up buttons, and silent alarm systems for financial and retail environments.

DMP Bosch Honeywell Residio

Structured Cabling & Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade Cat6 cabling, rack and stack deployments, IDF/MDF closet organization, demarc extensions, and patch panel termination. All drops tested, certified, and labeled to enterprise standards using Fluke DSX testers.

Cat6 Rack & Stack IDF/MDF Fluke DSX

Networking & Connectivity

Circuit extensions (T1, DSL, POTS), demarc extensions, PoE lighting deployment, VoIP/SIP system migrations, and cellular signal boost installations. Tier 2/3 troubleshooting for persistent network and hardware integration failures.

VoIP/SIP PoE OTDR Demarc Ext.

Project Management & SOW Development

Full project lifecycle oversight for multi-phase low-voltage installations: budget forecasting, Scope of Work (SOW) authoring, crew scheduling, procurement, timecard auditing, change order management, and client reporting.

SOW Budget Mgmt Risk Mitigation Scheduling

System Recovery & Remediation

Specialized auditing and correction of failed or non-compliant low-voltage installations. Trusted by major clients to identify critical errors from prior contractors and bring systems into 100% compliance with corporate and code requirements.

Audit Code Compliance Tier 3 Support Remediation
