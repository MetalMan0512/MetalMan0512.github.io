About Daniel

Trusted Low Voltage Systems Expert

Daniel Hillhouse is a Senior Project Manager and Low Voltage Systems Specialist with over a decade of hands-on experience delivering complex security and infrastructure projects for enterprise clients across California and throughout the Western and Southern United States — spanning grocery chains, big-box retail, banking, logistics, food service, data centers, and warehousing.

A licensed C-7 Low Voltage Contractor, Daniel has built much of his career as a trusted field lead and subcontractor for some of America's largest security integrators — representing their brand standards across statewide multi-site rollouts with no-rework results. He consistently earns a reputation as the technician teams call when a project needs to be rescued, accelerated, or done right the first time.

From directing multi-year enterprise security overhauls to terminating 10,000+ feet of Cat6 cabling in dense IDF closets, Daniel combines field-level technical mastery with project management discipline, always delivering on time, within budget, and to the strictest compliance standards.

  • Top-performing crew on a statewide enterprise grocery chain security overhaul: Phase 1 across 10+ high-volume retail locations
  • Appointed Lead Recovery Technician at a major financial institution to audit & rectify a failing multi-floor security installation
  • Commissioned 2 full big-box retail sites per day during a rapid-response statewide security panel rollout across California
  • 100% project completion rate across retail, food service, banking, and logistics sectors

Approach

Methodical from Assessment to Sign-Off
